Upbeat cinematic pop-rock with neon synths and driving bass, starting with a punchy intro riff that builds momentum. Verse introduces melodic hooks with syncopated drums and shimmering arpeggios. Outro fades with echoing synth pads and fading percussion, leaving a lingering sense of triumph.
